History
Back in the year 2012, Green coffee extract was promoted on the Dr. Oz Show. For those who don’t know, Dr. Oz is an American TV doctor and one of, if not the most popular health "guru" in the world. According to Dr Oz, a two weeks study was done on 100 women. Half of which were given 400 mg of green coffee bean extract 30 minutes before each meal. And the other half were given a placebo. After the two weeks, the green coffee bean extract group lost an average of 2 pounds (≈ 0.9 kg). While the placebo group lost an average of 1 pounds (≈ 0.45 kg) Ever since after that episode of Dr Oz show, Green coffee extract... BLEW UP!!
What Is Green Coffee Bean?
Green coffee beans have the ability to inhibit the absorption of fat, as well as stimulate the activation of fat metabolism in the liver, which in turn has a positive effect on weight loss. Also, green coffee contains in its composition antioxidants, vitamins and contributes to the improvement of mental and physical activity, which is also important in the fight against extra pounds. The main effect of the Green Coffee is to block the feeling of hunger and the subsequent saturation of the body. Main targeted actions are: reducing the absorption of carbohydrates from food, reducing the release of carbohydrates in the liver, and reducing blood sugar levels, which causes the body to use deferred fat reserves.

How To Prepare Green Coffee

  • Grind the green coffee beans, then take one glass of water 150ml or 1 sachet water. Put one table spoon of green coffee beans in the water and mix.
  • Put the mix content in a stove/gas or any heating means and bring to boil for about 8-10mins over low heat approx.
  • Sieve the health green coffee beverage and drink! For better taste add honey or cardamon only
